Now that you know what goes into a cover letter, you can write your own! First, select a job that you would like to write a cover letter for. This can be a job that you found an advertisement for or one that you would like to have! _________________________________________________________________________________ Lesson 11 – Breaking Down a Cover Letter   Remember, a cover letter is used to:
  • Introduce yourself.
  • Invite an employer to read your resume.
  • Highlight some of the most important qualities from your resume.
  • Request an interview.
  Writing the Body Paragraphs The body of a cover letter can seem intimidating! If you are stuck, breaking the letter down into small pieces can help. Here is an easy, step-by-step guide on what to include in your body paragraphs. These are the “bones” of each paragraph, but you may need to add more description to make your sentences flow together. _________________________________________________________________________________ Build Your Own Cover Letter When this is typed, it will all fit on one page! Introduction Paragraph __________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________ Checklist Did you……
  • State your reason for writing?
  • Name the position you are applying for?
  • Say how you found out about the job?
  Body Paragraph ________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________ Checklist Did you……
  • State at least two qualifications you have for this job?
  • Give specific examples of things you have done that make you qualified?
  • Use transferrable skills and active, professional language?
  • Say why you want to work in this field?
Closing Paragraph _______________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________   Checklist Did you……
  • Indicate that you would like an interview?
  • Include your contact information?
  • Say that you will be following up?
Thank them for their time? Sincerely, Great job! Here are some general guidelines for your cover letter:
  • Keep it to one page!
  • Use correct punctuation.
  • Make sure all words are spelled correctly.
  • All names, places, and companies have the first letter capitalized.
  • Capitalize the first letter in each sentence.
  • Make sure to sell yourself as a qualified applicant.
  • Be SPECIFIC about the job you are applying for and its requirements.
  • Don’t repeat your resume word for word.
