More on Resumes! So far, we have been learning the steps for finding a job. To recap:
  1. Find an available job
  2. Make first contact with an employer
  3. Send a resume and cover letter
  4. Interview
  5. Get a job offer!
Please follow through the next topics to learn how to write an effective resume and complete this course. _________________________________________________________________________________ Remember that the phone number you write on your heading is what an employer will be calling! Make sure that this phone number has a professional voicemail. _________________________________________________________________________________ Voicemail Etiquette Your voicemail should include:
  • Your first AND last name (or your phone number)
  • Saying that you are unavailable or busy
  • Asking the caller to leave a message
It should NOT include:
  • Swear words
  • Music
  • Slang
