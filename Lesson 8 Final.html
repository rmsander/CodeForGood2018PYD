_________________________________________________________________________________ Formatting a Resume Here are a few style tips to follow:
  • Use white paper! There are specific types of resume paper to make your resume look very professional! (But any white paper is okay.)
  • Use black ink and an easy-to-read font (usually Times New Roman or Arial).
  • Make sure everything is lined up on your resume. This means:
    • All dates are indented the same amount.
    • All s/ection headings, such as “Education,” are bold so they stand out.
    • Do not “center” align any of your text except maybe the heading.
  • Try to minimize white space! When you look at your resume from far away, the text should be evenly spaced around the page.
Other Tips:
  • It is helpful to use a resume template (such as the templates available in Microsoft Word®) to make a resume that looks nice and is easy to read.
  • Remember that employers don’t like to see resume errors, so make sure you proofread it several times and have at least one other person edit it!
